## AI Companion Relationship

These skills are designed to be used **with** AI companions — not as standalone tools. The companion relationship is itself a relational practice:

- The AI companion is oriented by the llms-txt guidance
- The human steward provides vision, context, and relational authority
- Together they practice the frameworks — creating, not just analyzing

This is the **EAST practice** described in [Prompt Decomposition](prompt-decomposition.md): intentions are explored collaboratively, then guided toward SOUTH practices of planning and action.
